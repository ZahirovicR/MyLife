Robert Zahirovic

I am a young man, native of Bosnia. I arrived in the US in 2000, and became paralyzed in 2007. I've learned through my life's journey that life is like a game of poker. If you get dealt a bad hand, you can fold or you can play.

My Old Life

The day after this picture was taken, I slipped on oil at a 4-way stop and lost control of my bike. This resulted in a T-12 and L-1 break in my spine. I thought my life was over, my opportunity to be a father to my son and relationship with my girlfriend (now wife).

While my life changed drastically following my motorcycle accident, things have only gotten better. I formed a closer bond with my son (now age 12) and married my wife in 2012. Furthermore, I began college as a student at JCTC. I bought a house, work and drive just as an able bodied person would do. I'm appreciative of the life I live and am even thankful for the accident as it opened up my eyes to all that I am thankful for, what's important in life, and has made me driven to a point that I never give up on anything I start and believe in.
